Agents write a shared environmental trace m_t. Later agents read this trace
through a network boundary B and update their policy pi_t.

m_{t+1}=rho m_t+W(a_t,B)
pi_{t+1}=P(pi_t,m_t,u_t)
action_t=A(pi_t,m_t,B)

The claim fails if trace erasure or network rewiring does not change the later
collective route choice.
